Welcome to TrekHouse, the home of the future!

We've seen the smart phone, and the smart car. Now it's time for the Smart Home. By taking advantage of exciting new web technologies, we're creating a house that is not only functional, but intelligent and responsive. Call people on your contacts list and play music with a simple voice command from anywhere in your house. Harness data-gathering abilities to anticipate your needs in real time. Link up your house to existing web technologies to further enhance and customize your experience.
